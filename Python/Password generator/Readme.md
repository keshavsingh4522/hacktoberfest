## What is the used of this app:
This a password generator app which create a strong password for your website/social media. You just have to put your website/social media name, your gmail and
click on <b>generate password</b> to create a random strong password to secure your account. After this click on <b>Add</b> to save your password with other info in a text formate, so for future you won't have to reset your password again
just go through your file and you will get the password.
